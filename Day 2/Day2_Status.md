## 💡 Key Learnings

- Odoo is available in two editions: Community and Enterprise.
- Community Edition is free, open source, and ideal for learning and development.
- Enterprise Edition is a paid version with advanced business features and official support.
- Both editions share the same core ERP platform.
- Choosing the right edition depends on business size and requirements.
