# 1. What is ERP?

**ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning)** is a business management software that integrates all departments of an organization into one centralized system.

Instead of using separate software for Sales, Inventory, HR, Accounting, and Purchasing, ERP connects everything through a single database.

### Main Advantages

- Centralized Database
- Real-time Information
- Better Decision Making
- Increased Productivity
- Reduced Manual Work
- Improved Collaboration
- Better Customer Service

---

# 2. What is CRM?

**CRM (Customer Relationship Management)** is software that helps businesses manage interactions with customers throughout the entire sales process.

CRM stores customer information, tracks leads, manages sales opportunities, schedules follow-ups, and improves customer relationships.

### CRM Features

- Lead Management
- Contact Management
- Sales Pipeline
- Customer Support
- Follow-up Tracking
- Email Integration
- Meeting Scheduling
- Customer History

---

# 3. Difference between ERP and CRM

| ERP | CRM |
|------|-----|
| Enterprise Resource Planning | Customer Relationship Management |
| Manages the entire business | Manages customer relationships |
| Used by all departments | Mainly used by Sales and Marketing |
| Focuses on internal operations | Focuses on external customer interactions |
| Includes Inventory, HR, Finance, Manufacturing | Includes Leads, Customers, Opportunities and Sales |
| Improves operational efficiency | Improves customer satisfaction and sales |

---

# 4. Why do Companies Use ERP?

Companies use ERP because it integrates all business operations into one platform.

Benefits include:

- One centralized database
- Faster business operations
- Better communication between departments
- Reduced duplicate data
- Accurate reporting
- Better inventory management
- Improved financial tracking
- Increased employee productivity
- Better decision making
- Business process automation

---

# 5. What is Odoo?

**Odoo** is an open-source ERP and CRM platform used by businesses to manage their operations.

It is built primarily using **Python** and **PostgreSQL** and provides many business applications in one system.

### Odoo Features

- Open Source
- Modular Design
- Web-Based
- Highly Customizable
- Python Framework
- Easy Integration
- Suitable for Small, Medium, and Large Businesses
